YOUTH TRANSITION PROGRAM

The Youth Transition Program empowers young individuals with in-demand digital skills to thrive in today's digital economy. Gain the knowledge, tools, and confidence to launch your career and stay ahead in a competitive world.

About Youth Transition Program

Nigeria's youth unemployment rate is at an all-time high, with millions of young people struggling to secure meaningful work opportunities. Youth Transition Program is designed to bridge this gap by equipping participants with the employability and digital skills needed to thrive in today's job market and entrepreneurial landscape.

Through expert-led training, mentorship, and hands-on learning, we empower you to build market-relevant skills, enhance your job prospects, and even create your own opportunities.

What You Will Learn

Youth Transition Program offers a comprehensive curriculum covering:

  • Digital Marketing - Learn how to build online brands and drive business success
  • CV Writing & LinkedIn Optimization - Position yourself for better job opportunities
  • Communication & Emotional Intelligence - Develop essential workplace skills
  • Time Management & Growth Mindset - Build habits for long-term career success
  • Practical Use of Digital Tools - Master tools that boost productivity and collaboration

Program Details

Duration - 2 Weeks

Mode of Training

Fully Virtual - All sessions will be held through interactive virtual classes on Zoom, ensuring a dynamic learning experience.

Who Can Apply

  • Young graduates
  • Final year students
  • Unemployed youth
  • NYSC Corps members
  • Youths and girls between the ages of 18-35 years who want to gain skills that give them an edge in the job market

Why join the Youth Transition Program

  • Hands-on Learning Practical training tailored for real-world impact
  • Career Acceleration Skills that make you more employable and ready for opportunities
  • Expert-Led Training Learn from industry professionals and mentors
  • Remote & Accessible Join from anywhere in Nigeria and learn from the comfort of your home
