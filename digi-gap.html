About DigiGap Program

The DigiGap Program, powered by NerdzFactory Foundation, is designed to equip young Nigerians with in-demand digital and technology skills. This program provides participants with the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to start and grow a successful tech career. With the increasing demand for digital professionals globally, DigiGap is your opportunity to bridge the gap between job seekers and emerging digital opportunities.

Why Join DigiGap?

Participants will:

  • Learn highly sought-after digital skills
  • Gain access to industry-relevant training
  • Develop soft skills essential for the workplace
  • Work on a capstone project for hands-on experience
  • Boost their employability and economic opportunities

Who Can Apply?

  • Young Nigerians aged 20-35 years
  • Unemployed or underemployed individuals looking to build a tech career
  • Minimum education level: Diploma
  • Passionate about technology and digital careers
  • Has internet access and can attend all training sessions

Program Structure

The DigiGap Program consists of;

  • Workshops & Webinars – Learn foundational digital skills
  • Specialized Bootcamp – Choose a learning track

Learning Track

  • Design - Graphics & Product Design
  • Digital Marketing Training Program

What Participants Will Gain

  • Job-ready skills in high-demand digital fields
  • Hands-on experience through project-based learning
  • Improved chances of employment and increased income
  • Opportunity to be part of a growing tech ecosystem
