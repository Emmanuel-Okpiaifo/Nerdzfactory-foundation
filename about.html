About Us

About Us

Nerdzfactory Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to equipping young adults across Africa with 21st century technology skills. NerdzFactory Foundation's mission is to catalyze every young African for a prosperous future.

At Nerdzfactory Foundation, we believe that every young person deserves a chance to succeed, regardless of their background as well as social or economic status. By collaborating with our partners and stakeholders, we are working to make a meaningful impact on the lives of Africa's most vulnerable population. Join us as we work together to build a brighter future for Africa.

Focus Areas

Our work

Youth Employment

Our programs empower young people with the tools and knowledge to thrive in the technology-driven job market, leading to economic empowerment.

Digital and Technology Education

We offer comprehensive training programs that equip young adults with the skills needed to succeed in today's digital world.

Disability Inclusion

We empower people with disabilities with the necessary tools and knowledge, while also seeking to break down barriers and create a more inclusive society where everyone has the opportunity to succeed.

Climate Change

We create awareness, promote sustainable practices, and support innovative solutions to mitigate the effects of climate change on humanity.

Entrepreneurship & Incubation

We believe that entrepreneurship is the bedrock of growth and development in society, hence our commitment to equipping young Africans with the skills, resources, and support needed to develop and launch their own businesses.

Research and Innovation

We promote a culture of innovation by supporting and promoting cutting-edge research, with the aim of producing solutions to society's most pressing challenges.
