The Boost Your Business Workshop, brought to you by NerdzFactory Foundation, through the support of Access, is designed to engage and empower women and youth-led micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs) to thrive in a competitive market

About BYB Workshop
  • The Boost Your Business Workshop is a virtual program set to empower women and youth-led micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs) with essential skills and tools necessary to scale, thrive, and drive sustainable business growth.
Key Modules
  • Sales Strategies for Business Growth
  • Financial Planning and Budgeting for Small Businesses
  • Cash Flow Management: Keeping Your Business Afloat
  • Leveraging Digital Marketing to Expand Your Reach
  • Staffing and Team Management for MSMEs
  • Understanding Business Compliance and Legal Requirements
  • Adopting Innovation and Technology for Business Success
