Boost Your Business (BYB) Workshop

The Boost Your Business Workshop is a tailored program designed to equip women and youth-led micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs) with essential business development skills. This program provides practical knowledge, expert guidance, and digital tools to help entrepreneurs improve financial management, enhance marketing strategies, and scale their businesses successfully.

REGISTER NOW



About BYB Workshop
  • Women and youth-led businesses play a crucial role in Nigeria's economic growth but often face challenges such as limited access to finance, digital skills, and business development resources. This workshop is designed to bridge that gap by providing structured business training to help entrepreneurs thrive in the digital economy.
Training Modules
  • Mastering Sales Strategies
  • Financial Planning & Budgeting
  • Cash Flow Management: Keeping Your Business Afloat
  • Leveraging Digital Marketing to Expand Your Reach
  • Staffing and Team Management for MSMEs
  • Business Compliance & Legal Requirements
  • Creating a Sustainable Business Model
  • Innovation & Technology Adoption
What Participants Will Gain
Through expert-led sessions, hands-on training, and mentorship, participants will gain the skills needed to:
  1. Boost sales and revenue
  2. Manage business finances effectively
  3. Leverage digital marketing for growth
  4. Expand market reach and sustainability
Program Details
  1. Duration: 6 Weeks
  2. Mode of Training: Fully Virtual
Program Benefits
  • Practical Training
  • Expert Guidance
  • Digital Empowerment
  • Support System & Community
  • Remote & Accessible
