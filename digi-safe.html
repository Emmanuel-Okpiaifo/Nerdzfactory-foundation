Digital Safety

Equipping Students with Essential Digital and Online Safety Skills

The Digital Safety Program is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to navigate the digital world safely. This program provides participants with practical strategies for online safety, responsible digital behavior, and cybersecurity awareness.

Why Join the Digital Safety Program?

With the increasing use of digital communication platforms such as WhatsApp, Telegram, Email, Facebook, and Snapchat among secondary school students, ensuring their online safety has become more important than ever. This program is designed to bridge the knowledge gap and provide students with essential online safety skills to protect themselves from cyber threats.

Through expert-led training, mentorship, and interactive learning, we empower students and teachers to adopt best practices in digital safety, fostering a more secure online environment for young individuals.

  • Expert-led training sessions
  • Practical cybersecurity strategies
  • Interactive learning experiences
  • Comprehensive digital safety education
  • Ongoing mentorship support

What You Will Learn

The Digital Safety Program offers a comprehensive curriculum covering:

  • Online Privacy & Security - Learn how to protect personal information online.
  • Cyberbullying Awareness & Prevention - Understand the dangers of cyberbullying and how to respond.
  • Recognizing Online Scams & Fraud - Identify and avoid common online scams.
  • Safe Social Media Practices - Develop responsible digital habits for social media use.
  • Digital Citizenship & Ethical Behavior - Foster positive interactions in online communities.
  • Empowering Teachers & Parents - Equip educators with strategies to teach digital safety effectively.

Program Details

  • Target Audience: Junior & Senior Secondary School Students
  • Locations: 20 Schools in Lagos and Ilorin
  • Number of Beneficiaries: 1,000 Students
  • Mode of Training: In-Person Training Sessions
  • Interactive Learning: Hands-on activities, discussions, and real-world case studies.

Why the Digital Safety Program is a Game-Changer

  • Increased awareness among students about online safety practices.
  • Improved ability to recognize and respond to online threats.
  • Empowered students who can confidently navigate digital spaces safely.
  • Stronger relationships between students, teachers, and parents on online safety discussions.
  • Long-term behavioral changes promoting responsible digital citizenship.
