About the PowerUp Program

Youth unemployment in Nigeria has been a persistent challenge, with rates fluctuating over recent years. According to the National Bureau of Statistics, the unemployment rate among youth aged 15-24 increased from 7.2% in Q2 2023 to 8.6% in Q3 2023. This upward trend underscores the pressing need for initiatives that equip young people with the skills and opportunities to secure meaningful employment.

The PowerUp Entrepreneurship Program addresses this issue by providing participants with essential entrepreneurial and business skills tailored to the current market landscape. Through expert-led training, mentorship, and hands-on learning, the program empowers young individuals to build sustainable businesses, enhance their leadership capabilities, and generate economic opportunities for themselves and their communities

What you will learn

The PowerUp Entrepreneurship Program offers a comprehensive curriculum covering:

  • Strategic Business Planning
  • Financial Management & Access to Capital
  • Social Media & Digital Marketing
  • Customer Acquisition & Retention
  • Pitching & Business Presentation Skills
  • Leadership & Growth Mindset
