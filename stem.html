STEM Educator
Immersion Program

The STEM Education Immersion Program is designed to equip teachers and aspiring educators with the skills and knowledge needed to enhance STEM education in Nigeria. This program provides essential training, tools, and resources to help educators deliver engaging, high-quality STEM instruction that prepares students for the future.

Learn More

Why Join the STEM Education Immersion Program?

The STEM Education Immersion Program equips teachers and aspiring educators with expert-led training, mentorship, and hands-on learning to enhance STEM education in Nigeria. Participants will:

  • Deliver engaging, inquiry-based STEM lessons
  • Integrate technology into classroom teaching
  • Apply real-world STEM concepts to improve student learning
  • Build a strong network of STEM educators

What You Will Learn

The STEM Education Immersion Program offers a well-rounded curriculum covering:

  • Inquiry-Based STEM Teaching Methods
  • Integrating Technology in STEM Education
  • Classroom Management & Engagement Strategies
  • Hands-on Experiments & Practical Applications
  • Developing a STEM Learning Community

Program Details

  • Duration: 3 Months
  • Mode of Delivery: Hybrid (Physical & Virtual Sessions)
  • Location: Lagos, Ogun state

Program Benefits

  • Hands-on Learning
  • Expert Guidance
  • Community Support
  • Flexible Learning
