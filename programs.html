About Us

At NerdzFactory Foundation, we are committed to equipping individuals with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities needed to thrive in today's digital economy. Through our diverse programs, we empower young people, entrepreneurs, and educators with essential digital, tech, and employability skills to drive innovation and economic growth.

Explore our programs and take the next step toward a brighter future.

The Youth Transition Program is designed to provide young people with employability and digital skills,to give them access to economic opportunities.For maximum impact.

Women Digital Entrepreneurship program

The Women Digital Entrepreneurship program (WDEP) is an initiative designed to equip women with the necessary skills and resources required to start and grow a business leveraging the internet.

PowerUp Entrepreneurship Program

The PowerUp Entrepreneurship Program is designed to equip young individuals with essential entrepreneurship skills to start, grow, and sustain successful businesses.

DigiGap Program

The DigiGap Program, powered by NerdzFactory Foundation, is designed to equip young Nigerians with in-demand digital and technology skills. This program provides participants with the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to start and grow a successful tech career.

Learn More

Boost Your Business Workshop

The Boost Your Business Workshop is a tailored program designed to equip women and youth-led micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs) with essential business development skills. This program provides practical knowledge, expert guidance, and digital tools to help entrepreneurs improve financial management, enhance marketing strategies, and scale their businesses successfully.

Learn More

STEM Education Immersion Program

The STEM Education Immersion Program is designed to equip teachers and aspiring educators with the skills and knowledge needed to enhance STEM education in Nigeria. This program provides essential training, tools, and resources to help educators deliver engaging, high-quality STEM instruction that prepares students for the future.

Learn More

Sustainability Clubs Program

The Sustainability Clubs Program is designed to equip students and teachers with knowledge and practical skills in environmental sustainability and climate action. This initiative fosters awareness, encourages responsible environmental practices, and empowers young individuals to become sustainability advocates in their schools and communities.
